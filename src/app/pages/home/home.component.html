What is Smart Fit?

Smart Fit is an AI-powered health and fitness app that helps you track habits, analyze meals, and understand your body.

How does the AI chatbot work?

The AI chatbot provides instant answers to your health, fitness, and nutrition questions based on smart analysis.

Is the body analysis accurate?

Our AI provides estimated insights based on your photo. While not medical-grade, it gives helpful guidance for tracking progress.

Can I track my daily habits?

Yes, you can track water intake, meals, tasks, and habits all in one place.

Do I need to pay to use Smart Fit?

Smart Fit offers free features with optional premium tools for advanced insights.

Is my data safe?

Yes, your data is securely stored and never shared without your permission.
